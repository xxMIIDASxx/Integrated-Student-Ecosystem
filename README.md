# EMSIGHT : a Unified Student Ecosystem

## 1. Project Presentation
**EMSIGHT (EMSI - Insight)** is a unified platform designed for the community of the [Ecole Marocaine des Sciences de l'Ingenieur (EMSI)](https://emsi.ma). The objective is to centralize existing digital services into a single, fluid, and intelligent ecosystem while introducing new collaborative and technological features.

---

## 2. Strategic Objectives
* **Centralization:** Group fragmented tools (Portal, Community, Resource Sharing) under a single interface.
* **Modernization:** Integrate Artificial Intelligence to assist in professional integration.
* **Collaboration:** Facilitate knowledge sharing among students through a participatory library.
* **User Experience (UX):** Reduce friction by avoiding multiple logins across different sites through a single unified platform.

---

## 3. Module Architecture

### A. Module "EMSIGHT Community" (Network and Career)
* **Reference:** Plateforme *EMSI Community* (www.emsicommunity.com).
* **Social:** News feeds, events, and interactions between students and alumni.
* **Added Value (AI CV Analyzer):** An automatic CV analysis tool using Natural Language Processing (NLP). The AI evaluates profile relevance against offers and suggests optimizations.

### B. Module "EMSIGHT Portail" (Academic Management)
* **Provenance des données:** *Portail Etudiant* (student.emsi.ma).
* **Academics:** Grade consultation, attendance tracking, and real-time schedules.
* **Added Value (Predictive Alerts):** Intelligent notification system warning students of unjustified absence risks or reminding them of important exam deadlines.

### C. Module "EMSIGHT Share" (Collaborative Resources)
* **Reference:** New internal module.
* **Document Repository:** Space allowing students to upload course notes and revision materials.
* **Filtered Search:** Filtering system by year, major, and subject for quick access.
* **Interactivity:** Rating system (stars) to highlight the most relevant resources.

---

## 4. Technical Specifications
* **Interface:** Unified dashboard with a Single Sign-On (SSO / Matricule) system.
* **Backend:** Robust database management for multi-source data aggregation.
* **AI:** Integration of artificial intelligence models for semantic analysis of resources.

---

## 5. Development Roadmap
1. **Phase d'Analyse:** Study of APIs and existing data structures (Design and Modeling).
2. **Core Development:** Creation of the main interface and integration of base modules.
3. **IA Implementation:** Development and training of the CV analysis module.
4. **Tests and Deployment:** Beta-testing phase with student groups.
